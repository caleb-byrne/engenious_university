A European company wants to expand its AI-powered customer service chatbot to handle customer data from EU residents. The company currently stores all customer conversation logs indefinitely and uses them for machine learning model training without explicit consent. They plan to implement personality profiling based on chat patterns and sell this data to marketing companies. The system also automatically makes credit decisions based on conversation analysis. What are the specific GDPR compliance issues with this approach, and what lawful basis would be required for each type of data processing? Please cite the relevant GDPR articles and explain the legal requirements for consent, data minimization, and purpose limitation.
